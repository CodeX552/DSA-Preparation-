# Graph Concepts — Fundamentals, BFS-DFS, Shortest Path, Topological-MST
## Representation: Adjacency List (List<List<Integer>>) preferred
## Types: Directed/Undirected, Weighted/Unweighted, Cyclic/Acyclic
## BFS: Level-order, shortest path (unweighted), multi-source
## DFS: Explore depth-first, cycle detection, connected components
## Dijkstra: Greedy + PriorityQueue, positive weights only
## Topological: DAG only, Kahn's (BFS) or DFS-based
## MST: Kruskal (sort edges + Union-Find) or Prim (PQ)
